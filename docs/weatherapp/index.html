Happy Weather

Even when the sky is gray and dreary, Happy Weather is sure to put a smile on your face! With bright colors and cute little faces, this app will make checking the forecast more friendly and approachable.

With its basic interface and simple graphics, Happy Weather allows you to quickly and easily catch up on current and future conditions whether at home, in your car, or out-and-about.

Features include:

  • Minimalist display
  • Temperature and conditions at-a-glance
  • Dynamic day-by-day scrolling
  • Material interface
  • Customizable widgets for desktop and Android
  • Happy little clouds!

Download today for mobile and desktop!
